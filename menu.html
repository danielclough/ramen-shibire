Broth was bomb. Noodles were bomb. Pork was bomb. Gyoza, bomb. Service, also bomb. This place did not disappoint!

One of a kind, their fun and creative take on ramen brings the heat back to Las Vegas! The orange spice level ramen had a unique flavor note of the habanero pepper, which nicely evolved at each bite (slurp).
